Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing how you use our site, and enabling certain features to work properly.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Basic functionality

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and customize your experience on our site.

  • Language preferences
  • Theme selection (light/dark mode)
  • Volume settings for audio players

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Page view statistics
  • Traffic sources
  • User behavior patterns

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help us deliver more relevant content and promotional materials.

  • Campaign effectiveness tracking
  • Social media integration
  • Personalized recommendations

Third-Party Cookies

We use services from these third parties that may place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics - For website analytics
  • PayPal - For processing donations
  • Spotify and Bandcamp - For music playback
  • Social media platforms - For content sharing

Cookie Management

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Use our cookie consent settings to choose which types of cookies you accept
  • Modify your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Use private browsing mode to prevent cookie storage

Please note that blocking certain cookies may impact the functionality of our website.
