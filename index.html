About the Project

Our Mission

Ambient Gaza is more than music‚Äîit's a bridge between cultures and a channel for humanitarian support. Through ambient soundscapes, we aim to raise awareness and provide direct aid to those affected by the crisis in Gaza.

The Story

Born from a desire to make a meaningful impact, this project combines the universal language of ambient music with direct humanitarian action. Each track incorporates authentic sounds from Gaza, transformed into contemplative ambient compositions.

The project brings together musicians, artists, and activists from around the world, united in their commitment to supporting Gaza through creative expression and direct aid.
